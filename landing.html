How Friendly Works

While there are many ways to find places to dine or attend a concert, there is no easy way to find out whether a business has the amenities you need to be comfortable. That’s why Friendly Like Me was created.  With each review, you help others find and support businesses that make fit and accessibility a priority.

Friendly Like Me is...

Precise

Friendly reviews ask people to measure the presence of important amenities with “yes or no.” This means you can find places specifically rated for the amenities you need, as well as those that are not suitable for you.

Personal

Build a profile to personalize your search based on what you care about and find places with the amenities you need to be comfortable.

Predictable

“Know before you go.” Ratings of a business’ accommodations are created by people with a viewpoint like yours who understand what it takes to measure accessibility, comfort, and fit.

Participation

When accessible places are easier to find, more people can take part. Friendly Like Me believes that when people with unique needs are empowered with accessibility information they will participate more - enabling each person to live their best, happiest, and healthiest life - today and every day.

POWERFUL!

Friendly Like Me leverages the power of community to drive change so that higher weight people can enjoy life with ease.
